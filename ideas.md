## Idea 1: Neo-Academic Glassmorphism (新学术毛玻璃主义)

**Design Movement**: Glassmorphism / Modern UI
**Core Principles**:
1.  **Transparency & Depth**: Reflecting the "multi-layered" nature of linguistic analysis (text, audio, video).
2.  **Clarity & Focus**: Content is king, but presented in a visually engaging, modern container.
3.  **Fluidity**: Smooth transitions representing the flow of language and data.

**Color Philosophy**:
-   **Primary**: Deep Indigo (`#4F46E5`) - Represents intelligence, technology, and depth.
-   **Secondary**: Soft Violet (`#A78BFA`) - Adds a creative, humanistic touch.
-   **Background**: Off-white with subtle, blurred gradient orbs in the background to create depth without distraction.
-   **Surface**: Translucent white cards with backdrop-blur, creating a "floating" feel.

**Layout Paradigm**:
-   **Asymmetric Grid**: Breaking the rigid academic grid. Text on one side, floating visual elements (icons, screenshots, abstract shapes) on the other.
-   **Z-Pattern Flow**: Guiding the eye naturally from the logo to the CTA, then through features to the bio.

**Signature Elements**:
-   **Frosted Glass Cards**: Used for feature highlights and download blocks.
-   **Gradient Orbs**: Subtle, moving background elements that shift slowly.
-   **Floating Icons**: 3D-rendered or high-quality flat icons that appear to float above the glass surfaces.

**Interaction Philosophy**:
-   **Hover Lift**: Cards gently lift and glow on hover.
-   **Parallax Scroll**: Background elements move at different speeds than foreground content.
-   **Smooth Reveal**: Elements fade and slide in as the user scrolls.

**Animation**:
-   **Hero Entrance**: Logo and text slide in from opposite sides; glass cards fade in.
-   **Scroll-triggered**: Feature cards cascade in.

**Typography System**:
-   **Headings**: `Outfit` or `Plus Jakarta Sans` - Geometric, modern, approachable sans-serif.
-   **Body**: `Inter` or `DM Sans` - Clean, highly readable for technical details.

## Idea 2: Swiss International Data (瑞士国际数据风)

**Design Movement**: Swiss Style / International Typographic Style
**Core Principles**:
1.  **Grid Systems**: Mathematical precision reflecting the analytical nature of the software.
2.  **Objective Photography/Imagery**: Letting the software screenshots and author photo speak for themselves.
3.  **Typography as Image**: Large, bold type used as a primary design element.

**Color Philosophy**:
-   **Primary**: Electric Blue (`#2563EB`) - High contrast, digital-native.
-   **Accent**: Vermilion (`#EF4444`) - Used sparingly for critical CTAs (Download).
-   **Neutral**: Stark White (`#FFFFFF`) and Jet Black (`#18181B`).
-   **Intent**: High contrast for maximum readability and a "tool-like" precision.

**Layout Paradigm**:
-   **Modular Grid**: Strict 12-column grid. Content blocks align perfectly.
-   **Split Screen**: Hero section split 50/50 between bold typography and a large, high-fidelity software interface mockup.

**Signature Elements**:
-   **Thick Dividers**: Bold horizontal lines separating sections.
-   **Oversized Typography**: Headlines that span the full width or break the grid intentionally.
-   **Monochrome Icons**: Simple, stroke-based icons.

**Interaction Philosophy**:
-   **Snap Scroll**: Sections snap into place for a structured reading experience.
-   **Micro-interactions**: Buttons have sharp, instant color inversions on hover.

**Animation**:
-   **Typewriter Effect**: For the main tagline.
-   **Staggered Reveal**: Grid items appear one by one.

**Typography System**:
-   **Headings**: `Helvetica Now` or `Roboto Flex` (Bold/Black weights) - Iconic, neutral, authoritative.
-   **Body**: `Roboto Mono` or `JetBrains Mono` - Hinting at the coding/data aspect of the tool.

## Idea 3: Organic Linguistic Flow (有机语言流)

**Design Movement**: Organic Modernism / Soft UI
**Core Principles**:
1.  **Natural Shapes**: Avoiding sharp corners; using blobs and waves to represent the fluidity of speech and language.
2.  **Warmth & Technology**: Bridging the gap between human language and machine analysis.
3.  **Storytelling**: Guiding the user through the "journey" of research.

**Color Philosophy**:
-   **Primary**: Teal (`#0D9488`) - Calming, intellectual, balanced.
-   **Secondary**: Coral (`#FB7185`) - Warm, energetic, human.
-   **Background**: Cream/Beige (`#FFF7ED`) - Easy on the eyes, paper-like feel.

**Layout Paradigm**:
-   **Single Column Stream**: A central flow of content with elements branching off organically.
-   **Curved Sections**: Section dividers are waves or curves, not straight lines.

**Signature Elements**:
-   **Blob Shapes**: Background shapes that morph.
-   **Soft Shadows**: Diffused, colored shadows rather than black/grey.
-   **Hand-drawn Accents**: Subtle underlines or arrows to direct attention.

**Interaction Philosophy**:
-   **Fluid Morphing**: Buttons change shape slightly on hover.
-   **Smooth Scroll**: A heavier, smoother scroll feel.

**Animation**:
-   **Wave Motion**: Section dividers gently undulate.
-   **Fade Up**: Content floats up gently.

**Typography System**:
-   **Headings**: `Merriweather` or `Lora` - A modern serif that feels academic yet warm.
-   **Body**: `Nunito` or `Quicksand` - Rounded sans-serif to match the organic shapes.
</text>
